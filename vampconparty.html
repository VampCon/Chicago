Description

VampCon is having its first pre-party, hosted by Count Gregula.

‡ We'll have a vampire costume contest, First place prize for the costume contest is a VampCon Hoodie and second place is a VampCon t-shirt.

‡ Convention ticket specials, special VampCon Pre-reg price of $45 (Only Available at this event)

‡ A raffle to win VIP passes (Raffle prize is a VampCon RIP Prize Package which includes VIP Tickets to VampCon, VampCon Celebrity guest autographed photos and more).

The event will be held at the Celtic Crown Public House on
Thursday, September 13th, 2018 from 6 PM - 10 PM

$5 admission (comes with 1 raffle ticket)
or
$15 admission (comes with 2 drink tickets and 2 raffle tickets)
